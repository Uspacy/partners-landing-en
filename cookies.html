INTRODUCTION

This Cookies Policy (“Policy”) is established by Uspacy LLC (“Company”, “Uspacy”, “us”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) to inform the users of the processing we carry out in relation to your personal data or the personal data you provide us with through the use of cookies.
We use cookies on our website https://ua.uspacy.partners (“Website”) for the purposes described in this Policy.

Cookies by provider

There are two types of cookies divided in accordance with the category of provider, namely:

  • First-party cookies: cookies that are placed on the Platform by the host of the domain the user is visiting which we have put in.
  • Third-party cookies: cookies that are placed on the Platform by the domains other than the one the user is visiting. As a variety of features necessary to ensure the personalised and convenient experience of the user may not be delivered by the Company itself, the third-party cookies are used.
We use both the first-party and third-party cookies.
Cookies by period of expiry

The cookies also vary depending on the period of retention, namely:

  • Session cookies: these are the temporary cookies, which are deleted when you leave the Platform.
  • Persistent cookies: these cookies stay in one of your browser's subfolders until you delete them manually or your browser deletes them based on the duration period contained within the persistent cookie's file.
YOUR CHOICES

You have the right to choose whether to accept the particular cookies or not. However, cookies are an important part of how our website works. Please be aware that if you choose to block or delete the particular cookies, some features of our website may not function well or may become unavailable.

Most web browsers are by default set to accept cookies. However, you can still choose to set your browser to block or delete cookies. To modify the settings of your browser, follow the instructions included in the following links:

  • Google Chrome (https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en);
  • Safari (https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac);
  • Opera (https://help.opera.com/en/latest/web-preferences/);
  • Microsoft Edge (https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/delete-and-manage-cookies-168dab11-0753-043d-7c16-ede5947fc64d);
  • Mozilla Firefox (https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer).

The list of third party’s cookies providers is not exclusive and it might change from time to time in course with our activity.

Most web browsers collect fingerprint data according to its own practices and policies. We do not process such data and are not responsible for its collection and processing.

Also, this Policy provides the list of cookies collected by our contractors by means of the Platform. We do not process such cookies. If you have any questions or requests regarding such cookies, you should address them to the respective contractor.

Please, bear in mind that this policy DOES NOT cover the cookies provided by the third parties when you follow the links to any website other than our Website.

TYPES OF COOKIES, PURPOSES AND RETENTION

We use four main types of cookies, including those placed by the third-party services that appear on our Website:

  • Necessary: enable users to use the website by providing basic functions, i.e. page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies;
  • Necessary: enable our website to remember choices that the user makes, i.e. language choices;
  • Statistical: help us to understand how users interact with our website (e.g. page visits and page load speed) by collecting information anonymously to avoid your identification.
  • Marketing: these cookies are used to track the visitor’s interest of a particular product, to measure the number of unique visits and recognise returning users and to deliver a targeted advertisement to users. These cookies are placed by us and selected third parties and enable adverts to be presented to you on third party websites.
LEGAL GROUNDS
